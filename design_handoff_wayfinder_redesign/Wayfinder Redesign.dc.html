How it works

From front door to final room, in three moves

No beacons, no hardware. Wayfinder fuses GPS, your steps and your compass to keep the blue dot moving — even where satellites can't see you.

Search any room

Type a ward, a shop or a street address. Indoor points and the outside world rank in one list, nearest first.

Follow the line

Turn-by-turn guidance across floors — lifts included — with live re-routing the moment you take a wrong turn.

Map it yourself

Walk an area or upload a floor plan — your route becomes the corridor layout, and signs are read straight from footage.
